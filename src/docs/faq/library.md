# Library FAQ

Learn how to manage your local manga collection effectively.

### How do I add manga to my library?
When browsing a manga in a source, simply click the **"Add to Library"** button on the Manga Detail page (represented by a heart or bookmark icon). The manga will instantly appear in your Library tab.

### Why do some manga show "NEW" badges?
The **NEW** badge automatically appears when Yomic detects that a manga in your library has published new chapters you haven't read yet. It uses smart detection to compare your read progress with the latest available chapters.
The badge will automatically disappear once you open the chapter or mark it as read.

### Why isn't my library updating automatically?
To save bandwidth and processing power, global auto-updates are handled by our **Smart Updates** algorithm. 
If a manga has been on hiatus or hasn't updated for a very long time, Yomic might skip it during a global update cycle. You can always force an update by pulling down or clicking the refresh button on the Manga Detail page.

### Can I organize my library into folders?
Yes! As of v1.5.0, you can use **Categories** to organize your library.
- Click the **Manage Categories** button in your Library to create custom folders.
- You can assign manga to one or multiple categories simultaneously.
- You can even exclude specific categories (like "Finished" or "Hiatus") from the global auto-update cycle to save time!
