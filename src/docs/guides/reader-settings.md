# Reader Settings

Yomic provides a highly customizable reading experience. You can access the reader settings by opening any manga chapter and clicking the settings icon.

## Reading Modes

You can choose from several reading modes to suit your preference:

- **Webtoon (Long Strip):** Images are displayed continuously, scrolling vertically. Ideal for webtoons.
- **Single Page:** Displays one page at a time. Click the sides of the screen or use the arrow keys to navigate.
- **Dual Page:** Displays two pages side-by-side, similar to a physical manga volume.

## Appearance

- **Background Color:** Change the background color behind the pages (e.g., Black, White, or Custom).
- **Scale Type:** Adjust how images fit on the screen (Fit Width, Fit Height, Original Size).

## Filters

You can apply custom color filters to the reader, which is useful for reading at night or reducing eye strain:
- Adjust brightness and contrast.
- Apply a color tint (e.g., warm/amber tint for night reading).
