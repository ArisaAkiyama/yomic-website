# Tracking

**Status:** 🚧 Currently in Development

Integrating Yomic with third-party tracking services (such as MyAnimeList, AniList, Kitsu, and MangaUpdates) is a highly requested feature and is currently on our roadmap for a future update.

Once implemented, this feature will allow you to:
- Automatically sync your read progress with your online lists.
- View community scores and detailed metadata.
- Import your existing online lists directly into Yomic's library.

For now, Yomic relies entirely on its internal database to track your reading history and library progress. We recommend generating regular [Backups](./backups.md) to ensure your progress is never lost!
