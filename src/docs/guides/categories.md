# Categories

By default, every manga you add to your library is dumped into a single, massive list. If your collection grows into the hundreds, it becomes difficult to track what you're currently reading versus what you've finished or dropped.

As of v1.5.0, Yomic introduced **Library Categories** (Custom Folders) to solve this.

## Managing Categories

1. Go to your **Library** tab.
2. Click the **Manage Categories** button.
3. A dialog will appear where you can Create, Rename, Delete, and Reorder categories.
4. Examples of good categories: *Reading*, *Completed*, *On Hold*, *Plan to Read*.

## Assigning Manga to Categories

When adding a new manga to your library, Yomic will prompt you with a **Category Selection Dialog**. You can select one or multiple categories for the manga to belong to.

For manga already in your library:
1. Open the Manga Detail page.
2. Click on the Category label (or the settings gear).
3. Check the boxes for the categories you want to assign it to.

## Update Exclusions

One of the most powerful features of Categories is the ability to exclude them from the [Smart Updates](../updates/smart-updates.md) engine.

If you edit a Category (like "Finished"), you can check the box labeled **"Exclude from global updates"**. Any manga placed inside this category will be ignored during background syncs, drastically improving performance and saving network bandwidth!
