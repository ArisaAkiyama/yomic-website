# Reader

Yomic provides a robust, native reader designed specifically for desktop platforms to deliver the most comfortable reading experience.

## Reading Modes

You can customize how chapters are displayed depending on the type of comic you are reading:

- **Webtoon Mode**: Specifically designed for long-strip comics (Manhwa/Webtoons). Images are loaded seamlessly in a continuous vertical scroll without any gaps between pages.
- **Manga Mode (Dual-Page Spread)**: As of v1.5.0, Yomic supports displaying two pages side-by-side! This mode utilizes **Right-to-Left** navigation to replicate the authentic experience of reading a physical Japanese manga.

## Fit and Scaling
You can adjust the image scaling by toggling the **FitScale** option.
- **Fit Width**: Scales the image to fit horizontally (default for Webtoons).
- **Fit Height (90%)**: Scales the image so the entire page is visible vertically with a slight margin, preventing cutoff.

## Navigation
The Reader is fully optimized for desktop usage:
- **Arrow Keys**: Use the Left/Right or Up/Down arrow keys to pan through images and transition between pages.
- **Auto-Transition**: Reaching the end of a chapter and pressing the "Next" key will automatically load and index the subsequent chapter based on your Library's SortOrder settings.
