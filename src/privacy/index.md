# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** June 24, 2026

## Overview

Yomic ("we", "our", or "us") is an open-source desktop application for reading manga, webtoons, and comics. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your data when you use the Yomic application.

## Data Collection

**Yomic does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.** 

The application operates entirely locally on your device. All data related to your library, reading progress, and settings are stored locally on your hard drive. 

## Network Activity

The application makes network requests only for the following purposes:
1. **Fetching Content:** To download manga information, chapters, and images from the extensions/sources you have installed and chosen to use.
2. **Checking for Updates:** To check the official GitHub repository for new releases of the application or extensions.

These requests are made directly from your device to the respective servers. Yomic does not proxy this traffic or track your activity.

## Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please open an issue on our [GitHub repository](https://github.com/ArisaAkiyama/yomic).
